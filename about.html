Dare to dream

At Circuit Mind we are completely automating the design of electronic circuit boards. Electronic circuit boards are ubiquitous, the cornerstone of today’s technologies and key to the future we all envision.
Yet today designing an electronic circuit board is an extremely manual, long and tedious process. At Circuit Mind we believe that this is wrong and we are on a mission to automate such process, thus tackling a £40bn global opportunity.
We are taking the lessons learned from the integrated circuit workflow (hardware description language, logic synthesis, automated place and route) to create an entirely new compiler for circuit boards. This compiler takes as inputs the circuit board requirements and outputs the circuit board design file, ready for manufacture.
